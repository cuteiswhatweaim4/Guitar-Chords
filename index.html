Basic Chords

C Major

Chord Diagram: ![C Major Chord](path-to-image)

Finger Position: 1st finger on 1st fret of the B string, 2nd finger on 2nd fret of the D string, 3rd finger on 3rd fret of the A string.

G Major

Chord Diagram: ![G Major Chord](path-to-image)

Finger Position: 2nd finger on 2nd fret of the A string, 3rd finger on 3rd fret of the E string, 4th finger on 3rd fret of the e string.

D Major

Chord Diagram: ![D Major Chord](path-to-image)

Finger Position: 1st finger on 2nd fret of the G string, 2nd finger on 2nd fret of the e string, 3rd finger on 3rd fret of the B string.
